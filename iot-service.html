IoT & Embedded Systems

From initial concept to mass production, ASRAGEN provides comprehensive IoT and embedded systems services. We specialize in building custom hardware, developing robust firmware, and integrating with cloud platforms to create intelligent, connected products that deliver real-world value.

Our End-to-End IoT Process

We follow a structured, agile approach to ensure your project's success at every stage:

  • Strategy & Discovery: We collaborate with you to define project goals, identify technical requirements, and create a clear roadmap for development.
  • Hardware Prototyping: Our engineers design custom PCBs, select optimal components, and build functional prototypes for rapid validation and iteration.
  • Firmware Development: We write clean, efficient, and reliable firmware using C/C++, Rust, and RTOS environments to control device behavior and ensure stable connectivity.
  • Cloud & Platform Integration: We seamlessly connect your devices to the cloud (AWS IoT, Azure, Google Cloud) for data collection, remote management, and advanced analytics.
  • Manufacturing & Deployment: We assist with DFM (Design for Manufacturing) and manage the transition from prototype to full-scale production.

Core Competencies & Technologies

Our team is proficient in a wide array of technologies essential for modern IoT solutions:

  • Microcontrollers: ESP32, STM32, nRF series, Raspberry Pi Pico
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth (BLE), LoRaWAN, Cellular (LTE-M/NB-IoT), MQTT
  • Sensors & Actuators: Integration of a wide range of sensors for environmental monitoring, motion detection, and more.
  • Cloud Platforms: AWS IoT Core, Azure IoT Hub, Google Cloud IoT
